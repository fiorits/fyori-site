VIDEO SUGGESTIONS
If you have an idea for a video, leave a comment in the suggestions megathread stickled at top of the subreddit. I sift through the comments at least once a month.
BACKGROUND MUSIC
All the music used in a given video is listed in the end credits and on the video's associated page on this website. Music that I've made myself can be found on my YouTube music channel and on SoundCloud. More information about my music can be found here.
SOCIAL MEDIA
The only social media platforms I use on a regular basis are Blusky and Reddit. There's also a fan-operated Discord server but I'm rarely online. I also have a few largely inactive accounts like Instagram and Twitch that I might find a use for at some point.
WEBSITE
I made this website. Expect bugs. It was made using regular ol' HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. The pages are served using a bare-bones Node server running Express and EJS. No CMS. No framework.
